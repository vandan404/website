About Us

Who we are

Vandan, CEO of the company, recently bought a EV. He realized how the current manufacturers of EVs don't use enviormental friendly batteries. The batteries are made of lithium which are a major cause of pollution. What our company plans to do is store hydro powered energy and use water as an energy source. The cars will generate hydro electricity in their bonet and reuse the water preventing wastage.

State of EVs currently

It's no more hidden from anyone that the Li-ion battery in electric vehicles is built to last till 6-7 years or hardly 8 years and after the battery decay period of an electric vehicle battery its user remains with no other choice than to buy a newer battery which costs nearly 3/4 th of the whole vehicle cost.
